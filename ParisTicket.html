Paris Je T'aime - Tourism Office

Discover Paris at your leisure, with Paris je t'aime!

Open your eyes and admire the iconic Eiffel Tower, the Arc de Triomphe or the Sainte-Chapelle... Embark on a fascinating boat cruise on the Seine where cathédrale Notre-Dame, the Grand Palais or the Hôtel de Ville reveal themselves from a new angle. On foot, by bike or by public transport, the monuments are all around you. Rich in culture, the museums and theatres await you, from the musée du Louvre to the centre Pompidou, not forgetting Parisian cabarets like the Moulin Rouge or Paradis Latin.

Looking for adventure? Explore the secrets of the capital: street art, immersive experiences, and gourmet cooking classes. Families can enjoy Cité des Sciences, the Grande galerie de l'Evolution, or the musée de l'Illusion.

To simplify your stay, opt for the Paris Passlib', the official tourism pass. It includes museum visits, Seine cruises, bike rides, and more. Choose from four formulas adapted to your stay.

Thanks to our partner activities, let yourself be enchanted by the Magic of Paris and experience unforgettable moments.
