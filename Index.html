Swimming in the Seine: a first-ever opening to
the public on 25 July in Paris!

From 5 July 2025, Paris will reopen swimming in the Seine with three
iconic sites accessible to the public: Bercy, Bras Marie and Bras de
Grenelle. This is a major event, a legacy of the 2024 Paris Olympic
Games.

A total of 11 free, supervised swimming areas await you all summer
long in the Seine and elsewhere, from the Bassin de la Villette to the
Canal Saint-Martin, including sports centres.
